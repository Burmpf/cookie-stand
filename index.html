About Us

Pat's Salmon Cookieshas been a family run business since 1776. Based out of Boston Massachusets, we strive to provide the tastiest and fishiest cookies the whole family will love. 100% of the procedes from our sales to towards bear and otter hunting. These efforts are to defend the salmon who can't defend themselves. Pat's has a hunting team dedicated to hunting down the predators of salmon.
