About Us

Pat's Salmon Cookieshas been a family run business since 1776. Based out of Boston Massachusets, we strive to provide the tastiest and fishiest cookies the whole family will love. 100% of the procedes from our sales to towards bear and otter hunting. These efforts are to defend the salmon who can't defend themselves. Pat's has a hunting team dedicated to hunting down the predators of salmon.



About our CEO: Pat the 6th



Pat, like all other Pats' before him, were raised with a deep respect for our fishy friends and a deep hatred for the animals who prey on them. Pat the 6th was the first Pat to take actions against their adversaries. He formed the B.O.S.S. (Bear and Otter Surgical Strike-team) in 2008 in an effort to support a good cause and to employ hunters during a difficult time in our economy.
